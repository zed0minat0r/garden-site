What to plant right now

The last frost date for our zone has passed. Here is what our growers are putting in the ground this week.

Plan Your Visit
Summer Color

Dahlias & Summer Bulbs

April is the window for tubers. Plant dahlia tubers now for spectacular late-summer blooms. Gladiolus and canna lilies also go in this month.

Annuals

Petunias & Impatiens

Spring annuals are at peak selection. Fill window boxes, containers, and beds now. Impatiens love shade; petunias take full sun all summer.

Natives

Native Pollinators

Black-eyed Susans, coneflowers, and bee balm are ready now. Native perennials establish fast in PA soil and return stronger every year.

Trees & Shrubs

Shade Trees & Ornamentals

Spring is the best planting window for trees. Roots establish before summer heat. Maples, dogwoods, and flowering shrubs in stock now.
