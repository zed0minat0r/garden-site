What to plant right now

The last frost date for our zone has passed. Here is what our growers are putting in the ground this week.

Dahlias & Summer Bulbs

April is the window for tubers. Plant dahlia tubers now for spectacular late-summer blooms. Gladiolus and canna lilies also go in this month.

Summer Color

Petunias & Impatiens

Spring annuals are at peak selection. Fill window boxes, containers, and beds now. Impatiens love shade; petunias take full sun all summer long.

Annuals

Native Pollinators

Black-eyed Susans, coneflowers, and bee balm are ready to go in now. Native perennials establish fast in PA soil and come back stronger every year.

Natives

Shade Trees & Ornamentals

Spring is the best planting window for trees. Roots establish before summer heat. Our nursery is stocked with maples, dogwoods, and flowering shrubs.
