Our story

A family garden,
a community home

"We're not a big-box store and we never will be. Every plant we sell, we stand behind."

— Eric Schmidt, Owner

For 58 years, Colonial Gardens has been where Phoenixville comes to dig in. Owner Eric Schmidt carries on a hands-on family legacy — no shortcuts, no corporate playbook, just real expertise and plants that last.

Come walk the acres of covered and uncovered greenhouses, talk to staff who genuinely love what they grow, and find exactly what your yard needs.

What’s in season

Spring 2026

Annuals & Perennials

Hundreds of varieties just arrived. Petunias, impatiens, coneflower, black-eyed Susan — all ready to go home with you. Peak selection is right now, before the Memorial Day rush.

Now Available

Vegetables & Herbs

Tomatoes, peppers, basil, thyme — get your kitchen garden going while the soil is still cool. Our growers start these on-site so they are already acclimated to Chester County conditions.

Spring Planting

Trees & Shrubs

Spring is the ideal planting window. Roots have until summer heat to establish. We stock maples, dogwoods, ornamentals, and flowering shrubs — all containerized and ready to go home today.
