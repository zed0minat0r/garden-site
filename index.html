Our story

Independent since
day one

Colonial Gardens has been a fixture in the Phoenixville community for 58 years. Owner Eric Schmidt carries on a family legacy of independent, passionate horticulture — no big-box shortcuts, no corporate playbook.

Acres of covered and uncovered plant shopping, a dedicated staff that actually loves plants, and a deep commitment to sustainability make this the kind of place you come back to every season.
